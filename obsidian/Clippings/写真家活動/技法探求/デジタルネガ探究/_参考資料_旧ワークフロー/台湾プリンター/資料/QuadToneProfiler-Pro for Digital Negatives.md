### What makes this new version so great?

**Here’s a quick example:**

The Marble Canyon picture above is a scan of a 9x13-inch albumen print from a PiezoPro dual-quad negative made with the new software. I mixed fresh silver nitrate and sensitized three 11x15-inch sheets of albumen paper in the morning, and I made the print above on the 3rd sheet of paper that same afternoon. I used two halves of the first sheet for the partitioning and linearization. I made two test prints of different negatives on the second sheet to play with other toners and then made this print on the third.
