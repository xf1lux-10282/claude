## Great photographs are made when equipment and technique fade to the background and intuition takes over

What I value and teach here goes beyond formulaic plugin settings, gimmicks and quick tricks. We'll dive deep into the aesthetic considerations that make great photographs using tools and resources I've learned or personally developed over the past decade scanning, editing, and printing to the highest possible standards.
