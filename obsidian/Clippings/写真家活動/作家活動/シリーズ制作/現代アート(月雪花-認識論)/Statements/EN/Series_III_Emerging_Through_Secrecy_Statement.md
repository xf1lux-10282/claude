# III. Emerging Through Secrecy
## Series Statement

**Daisuke Kinoshita**
**February 26, 2026**

---

## Condition of Reception

Sometimes, even when looking at a flower, one cannot identify it.
Yet it exists.

How a flower is received does not reside within the flower itself.
It emerges through interpretive structures.

When frameworks waver, objects do not immediately fix.
They are visible, yet cannot be identified.

Light quantity destabilizes reception.
Reception is not given—it is structured.

---

## Technical Information

**Medium**: Platinum/Palladium prints
**Sizes**: 8×10 inches to 16×20 inches
**Number of works**: 10-15
**Production period**: 2020–ongoing

**Material rationale**:
Platinum absorbs light. The image recedes into the paper.
Reception is never fully fixed.
