# I. Appearing Through Darkness
## Series Statement

**Daisuke Kinoshita**
**February 26, 2026**

---

## Condition of Visibility

The moon does not illuminate everything.
Yet it remains visible.

Visibility does not emerge under total illumination, nor in complete darkness.
It arises only within the relation between light and darkness.

What is seen is supported by what is unseen.
What appears is partial, structured by absence.

Light quantity destabilizes visibility.
Visibility is not given—it is constructed.

---

## Technical Information

**Medium**: Platinum/Palladium prints
**Sizes**: 8×10 inches to 16×20 inches
**Number of works**: 10-15
**Production period**: 2019–ongoing

**Material rationale**:
Platinum absorbs light. The image recedes into the paper.
Visibility is never fully fixed.
