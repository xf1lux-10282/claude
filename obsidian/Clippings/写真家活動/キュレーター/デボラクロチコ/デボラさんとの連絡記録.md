This would be a wonderful honor and significant opportunity.

Recently, I traveled to Taiwan for a platinum printing technical exchange. I am maintaining ongoing contact with them.

As for the exhibition opportunity in France that we discussed, I must admit that progress has been slower than I had hoped. I continue to explore this possibility, but it remains challenging.

None of these opportunities would have been possible without your thoughtful introduction to Ms. Aono and your continued guidance throughout my journey. Your support continues to open doors and create meaningful connections that I could never have imagined on my own.

I will keep you updated on further developments with all of these opportunities.

Thank you again for your invaluable mentorship and support.

With deepest gratitude and warm regards,

Daisuke Kinoshita
